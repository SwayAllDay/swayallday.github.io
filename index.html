About ZOYA

ZOYA is a Bulgarian electronic artist, producer, singer-songwriter and DJ known for blending cinematic electronic production with emotional songwriting and immersive sound design. Her music moves between progressive house, melodic dance music and modern pop, creating a signature sound that balances intimacy with festival-scale energy. Through each release and performance, ZOYA continues to build a cinematic and emotionally driven world that connects deeply with listeners around the globe.
